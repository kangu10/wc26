Methodology
  • Routes connect each team's consecutive match cities with geodesic lines between stadium coordinates.
  • Consecutive matches in the same city stay in the timeline, but add 0 km and 0 CO2e.
  • Airport access, training bases, ground transfers, aircraft type and actual charter routing are not included.
  • CO2e is estimated as route distance times US EPA passenger-mile air-travel factors times a 53-person delegation assumption based on FIFA's final release list limit: up to 26 players and up to 27 team officials.
Data Sources
  • TheStatsAPI: fixtures, teams, stages, stadiums.
  • FIFA World Cup 26 Regulations: final release list size used for the delegation assumption.
  • US EPA GHG Emission Factors Hub: passenger-mile air-travel emission factors.
